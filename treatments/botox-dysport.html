Botox & Dysport

Precision neurotoxin placement that softens dynamic lines while preserving your natural expressions.

The Treatment

Soften Lines,
Keep Your Expression

Botox and Dysport are FDA-approved neuromodulators that relax the small facial muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles — the lines that form between your brows, across your forehead, and around your eyes when you smile, squint, or frown.

At Lumina, every neurotoxin treatment is performed by a board-certified physician who maps your unique muscle activity before a single unit is injected. The goal is never a frozen look — it's softened lines and a rested, refreshed appearance that still moves and expresses naturally.

What It Treats

  • Frown lines between the brows (the "11s")
  • Horizontal forehead lines
  • Crow's feet around the eyes
  • Bunny lines on the nose
  • Lip flip & gummy smile correction
  • Jawline slimming & masseter reduction
