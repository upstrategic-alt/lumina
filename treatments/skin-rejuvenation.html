Skin Rejuvenation

PRP, microneedling, and medical-grade peels harnessing your body's own regenerative power.

The Treatment

Activate Your Skin's
Natural Renewal

Skin rejuvenation treatments work with your body's own healing response rather than against it. Microneedling creates controlled micro-channels that trigger collagen production; PRP ("vampire facial") concentrates your own platelet-rich plasma to accelerate repair; medical-grade peels exfoliate to reveal fresher skin beneath.

These modalities are often combined into a single session — microneedling with PRP infusion, for example — for compounding regenerative effects tailored to your skin's specific needs.

What It Treats

  • Fine lines & early aging
  • Acne scarring & large pores
  • Dull, uneven texture
  • Mild hyperpigmentation
  • Loss of skin firmness
  • General radiance & glow maintenance

Frequently Asked

Microneedling alone stimulates collagen via micro-injury. Adding PRP — plasma drawn from your own blood — infuses growth factors into those channels for faster, more pronounced healing and glow.
Most patients see best results from a series of 3–6 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart, followed by quarterly maintenance.
Expect redness similar to a mild sunburn for 24–48 hours. Most patients return to normal activities the next day with mineral makeup.
Many patients alternate skin rejuvenation series with periodic laser resurfacing for layered, long-term texture improvement.
