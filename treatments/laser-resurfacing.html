Laser Resurfacing

Fractional CO₂ and Fraxel treatments erasing years of sun damage, scars, and textural irregularities.

The Treatment

Reveal Renewed,
Resurfaced Skin

Laser resurfacing uses controlled, fractionated light energy to remove damaged outer skin layers and stimulate fresh collagen production beneath. The result is smoother texture, tighter pores, and a visible reduction in sun damage, scarring, and fine lines.

We offer both fractional CO₂ (for deeper, more dramatic correction) and Fraxel (for gentler, progressive renewal with less downtime), tailored to your skin type and recovery tolerance.

What It Treats

  • Sun damage & hyperpigmentation
  • Acne scarring & surgical scars
  • Fine lines & textural roughness
  • Enlarged pores
  • Uneven skin tone
  • Early signs of skin laxity
