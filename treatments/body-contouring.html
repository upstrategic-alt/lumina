Body Contouring

CoolSculpting and Emsculpt NEO technology sculpting your ideal silhouette without surgery or downtime.

The Treatment

Sculpt Without
Surgery or Downtime

CoolSculpting uses controlled cooling to crystallize and naturally eliminate stubborn fat cells, while Emsculpt NEO combines radiofrequency heating with high-intensity electromagnetic muscle stimulation to build muscle and reduce fat simultaneously.

Both technologies are FDA-cleared, non-invasive, and require zero downtime — making them ideal for patients who want visible body refinement without surgery or recovery time.

What It Treats

  • Abdomen & flank fat reduction
  • Bra & back fat
  • Inner & outer thigh contouring
  • Double chin (submental fat)
  • Core & glute muscle toning
  • Post-pregnancy body restoration
