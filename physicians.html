Our Physicians

Board-certified expertise, continuously trained in the most advanced aesthetic techniques available.

Dr. Elaine Chen, MD
Medical Director · Board-Certified Dermatologist

Dr. Chen founded Lumina in 2014 after a decade in academic dermatology. Renowned for her precise, natural approach to neurotoxin and filler artistry, she has trained injectors across the country and personally oversees every physician on staff.

Dr. Marcus Reyes, MD
Board-Certified Facial Plastic Surgeon

Dr. Reyes brings a surgeon's understanding of facial anatomy to every non-surgical treatment, specializing in advanced filler techniques, jawline contouring, and laser resurfacing protocols.

Dr. Priya Nair, MD
Board-Certified Internal Medicine · IV Therapy Lead

Dr. Nair leads our wellness and IV therapy program, designing custom nutrient protocols grounded in clinical evidence rather than trend.

Sofia Marchetti, RN
Master Injector · Aesthetic Nurse

With over 8,000 injections performed under physician supervision, Sofia is known among clients for an exceptionally gentle hand and meticulous symmetry.

James Whitfield, LE
Master Aesthetician · Skin Rejuvenation

James specializes in microneedling, PRP, and medical-grade peel protocols, building long-term skincare plans tailored to each client's skin journey.

Hana Kobayashi
Patient Care Coordinator

Hana guides every new patient through their first consultation, treatment planning, and financing options — your first point of contact at Lumina.
