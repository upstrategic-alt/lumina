How We Began

Founded on a
Simple Belief

Lumina Medical Spa opened its doors in Beverly Hills in 2014 with a singular mission: to practice aesthetic medicine the way it should be practiced — by board-certified physicians, never mid-levels, using only FDA-approved treatments, with zero pressure to upsell.

Over twelve years, that founding philosophy has shaped everything about how we operate. We've performed more than 14,000 treatments, but we still begin every single one with the same thorough, honest consultation we gave our very first patient.

Today, Lumina is home to a small team of physicians and master aestheticians who train continuously to stay at the forefront of aesthetic medicine — because your results deserve nothing less than mastery.
