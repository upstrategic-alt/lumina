Flexible Financing

Beautiful, radiant skin shouldn't be out of reach. We offer several ways to make treatment plans fit your budget.

CareCredit

A healthcare credit card accepted at Lumina for treatments of $200 or more. Choose from promotional no-interest plans of 6, 12, or 18 months, subject to approval.

Cherry

A simple, fast online application with monthly payment plans for aesthetic treatments — most approvals are instant and don't affect your credit score to check.

Lumina Loyalty

Earn points on every visit, redeemable toward future treatments. Members also receive early access to seasonal promotions and complimentary touch-ups on select services.

How It Works

Getting Started

1. Consult. Meet with one of our physicians for a complimentary consultation and treatment plan.

2. Choose your plan. Our patient care coordinators will walk you through CareCredit, Cherry, or a custom payment schedule based on your treatment plan total.

3. Apply in minutes. Most financing applications are completed on a tablet in our office and approved within minutes.

4. Begin treatment. Once approved, we'll schedule your first session — many plans allow treatment to begin the same day.
