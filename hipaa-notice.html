HIPAA Notice

Notice of Privacy Practices for Protected Health Information

Effective date: June 29, 2026

This Notice Describes

How medical information about you may be used and disclosed, and how you can get access to this information. Please review it carefully. Lumina Medical Spa is required by the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) to maintain the privacy of your protected health information (PHI) and to provide you with this notice of our legal duties and privacy practices.

How We May Use and Disclose Your PHI

  • Treatment. We use your health information to provide, coordinate, and manage your care, including consultations with physicians and aestheticians involved in your treatment plan.
  • Payment. We may use and disclose PHI to obtain payment for services, including billing and processing financing applications.
  • Healthcare Operations. We may use PHI for quality assessment, staff training, and practice administration.
  • Required by Law. We may disclose PHI when required by law, such as in response to a court order or public health reporting requirement.

Uses Requiring Your Authorization

Other than as described above, we will obtain your written authorization before using or disclosing your PHI — including for marketing purposes or before using identifiable before-and-after photographs in any promotional material.

Your Rights

  • The right to inspect and obtain a copy of your medical record.
  • The right to request corrections to your record.
  • The right to request restrictions on certain uses and disclosures.
  • The right to receive an accounting of disclosures of your PHI.
  • The right to request confidential communications by alternate means.
  • The right to receive a paper copy of this notice upon request.

Our Responsibilities

We are required by law to maintain the privacy of your PHI, provide you with this notice describing our legal duties and privacy practices, and abide by the terms currently in effect. We reserve the right to change our privacy practices and will notify you of material changes.

Complaints

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may file a complaint with our Privacy Officer at hello@luminaspa.com, or with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights. We will not retaliate against you for filing a complaint.
