Skincare That
Supports Your Results

What you do at home matters as much as what happens in our treatment rooms.

Skin Care · 8 Min Read · By James Whitfield, LE

A great in-office treatment can be undone — or amplified — by what you do at home. Here's the routine framework our aesthetician team recommends to every patient, regardless of which treatment you've had.

Before Any Treatment

  • Stop retinoids and exfoliating acids 3–5 days prior to laser, peel, or microneedling appointments.
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning for at least one week before resurfacing treatments.
  • Skip blood-thinning supplements (fish oil, high-dose vitamin E) for a few days before injectables to reduce bruising risk — always check with your physician first.

The Daily Non-Negotiable: SPF

If we could mandate one product, it would be a broad-spectrum SPF 30+ every single morning, reapplied if you're outdoors. UV exposure is the single biggest accelerant of skin aging and the fastest way to undo the benefits of laser resurfacing or skin rejuvenation treatments.

After Injectables (Botox & Filler)

  • Avoid touching or massaging the treated area for 24 hours.
  • Skip intense exercise and saunas the day of treatment.
  • Stay upright for 4 hours post-Botox to prevent product migration.

After Resurfacing or Microneedling

  • Use only gentle, fragrance-free cleansers for the first week.
  • Hold off on retinoids and active acids until fully healed (typically 1–2 weeks).
  • Keep skin consistently moisturized to support the healing barrier.

Every patient leaves Lumina with a written aftercare guide specific to their treatment — but if you ever have a question between visits, our care team is one phone call away.
