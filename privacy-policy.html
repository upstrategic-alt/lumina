Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Overview

Lumina Medical Spa ("Lumina," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website at luminaspa.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide when requesting a consultation, such as your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Usage data collected automatically, including pages visited, browser type, and device information, via standard analytics tools.
  • Communications you send us by phone, email, or contact form.

We do not collect protected health information (PHI) through this website. Medical history and treatment records are collected only in person or through our secure patient portal and are governed by our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to consultation requests and scheduling inquiries.
  • To send appointment reminders and practice updates, where you've opted in.
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate our website and scheduling systems (such as hosting and email providers), each bound by confidentiality obligations.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to understand site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Your Choices

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us at hello@luminaspa.com or calling (310) 555-0180. You may also request access to or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law.

Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be directed to hello@luminaspa.com or (310) 555-0180, or by mail to 436 N Bedford Dr, Suite 200, Beverly Hills, CA 90210.
